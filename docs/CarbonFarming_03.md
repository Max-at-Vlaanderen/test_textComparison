Carbon farming refers to agricultural practices aimed at capturing and storing [soil carbon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/soil_carbon) dioxide (CO2) from the atmosphere in soil and vegetation. This approach not only helps mitigate climate change by reducing greenhouse gas levels but also enhances soil health and productivity.
